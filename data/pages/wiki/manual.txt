====== Operating Manual ======

Polycademy encourages you to contribute to this wiki with your learned knowledge. The more information here the better for all students. Please learn the below documentation for the syntax and operation of Polycademy's wiki. In the future, there will be more sections added to reflect any technology we use to deliver and collaborate.

====== Dokuwiki ======

To edit the pages of the Codex, you need to learn Wiki syntax. It is similar but different to Wikipedia's Mediawiki syntax. Check the [[Syntax]] page for documentation.
